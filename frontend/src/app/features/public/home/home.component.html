About DSRiding

DSRiding is South Africa's leading platform for dressage competition management, designed to streamline the entire competition lifecycle from entry to results.

Our platform serves riders, clubs, show holding bodies, and officials across all nine provinces, providing a centralized system for managing competitions, entries, payments, and results.

  • Comprehensive competition management
  • Secure online payment processing
  • Real-time results and rankings
  • Document management and reminders
